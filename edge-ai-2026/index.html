Why we built it.

Entropora ships heydict, an on-prem voice agent for customers who cannot send audio into a hyperscaler. Mapping the European edge-AI landscape was part of understanding our own stack. Most of what we learned is not in any single public dataset. So we wrote the map we needed, then opened it.

If you build, invest in, or research this space: take the numbers, push back on the ones we got wrong, and tell us what you see that we missed. Reply on LinkedIn or email as@entropora.com.

Methodology. 81 companies in the core dataset, listed incumbents excluded. Funding figures as disclosed. European Edge-AI VC timeseries reconstructed from Dealroom Future-of-Compute + Sifted + Atomico; directional, not a census. Currency normalised to April 2026 USD. Data cut-off 19 April 2026.
